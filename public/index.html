About Me

Curious. Problem-solver. Empathetic.

I am a strong believer of Big Data and Machine Learning due its potential to bring about innovative products and make organizations more efficient. Data Science and Machine Learning are known buzz terms, but undeniably will be part of our future. I am currently pursuing a M.S. in Data Science degree at Columbia University to solidify my foundation in Statistics and Machine Learning.

When I'm not solving data science problems, I love to create websites, webapps, and interactive data visualizations for organizations I believe in (and sometimes just for fun!).

I am currently looking for an internship for Fall 2019 and a full-time job starting January 2020, so contact me for any kind of work opportunity. If you just want to connect and talk about tech, send me a message too!
